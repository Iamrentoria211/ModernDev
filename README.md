# ModernDev

> **Learn to code the right way — with structure, accountability, and AI that teaches, not codes for you.**

ModernDev is a subscription-based learning platform built for aspiring developers who want to break into tech without falling into tutorial hell or becoming dependent on AI to write their code. It combines structured curricula, role-based roadmaps, real project assignments, and AI-powered interview simulation — giving learners the experience of a coding bootcamp, the depth of a university course, and the accountability of a real job.

---

## The Problem

The internet has more free coding resources than ever — yet more developers feel lost than ever. The reasons are always the same:

- **Tutorial hell** — watching videos endlessly without building anything real
- **Vibe coding** — using AI to generate code they don't understand
- **No structure** — jumping between random courses with no clear path
- **No accountability** — no deadlines, no feedback, no one checking your work
- **No interview prep** — finishing courses but having no idea how to get a job

ModernDev solves all of this in one place.

---

## What ModernDev Does

ModernDev is a guided, AI-assisted learning platform with guardrails — meaning AI helps you learn, but never does the work for you. Every lesson ends with a task, quiz, or project that must be completed before moving forward. Just like a real school.

---

## Core Features

### AI Interview Simulator
Practice real technical interviews with an AI that simulates actual company interview scenarios. Get asked the questions real interviewers ask, receive feedback on your answers, and build the confidence to walk into any tech interview prepared.

### Structured Curriculum
Curated courses, PDFs, assignments, and projects — organized so learners always know what to study next. No overwhelming choices. No random jumping between topics. A clear path from zero to job-ready.

### Skill-Based & Role-Based Roadmaps
Choose your destination first — Frontend Developer, Backend Developer, Full Stack, DevOps, and more. ModernDev gives you a personalized roadmap based on where you are and where you want to go.

### Project-Based Learning
Every milestone ends with a real project, not just a quiz. Learners build a portfolio of actual work they can show to employers — not just certificates.

### AI-Assisted Learning with Guardrails
AI is available to help explain concepts, give hints, and answer questions — but it will not write your code for you. The goal is understanding, not output. Guardrails ensure that learners engage with the material deeply before moving on.

### Assignment & Quiz System
After every lesson, learners complete tasks, quizzes, or mini-projects that must pass before unlocking the next module. No skipping. No passive consumption.

### Progress Tracking & Project Management
A personal dashboard that shows where you are, what's next, what you've completed, and how far you've come. Visual progress keeps learners motivated and accountable.

## Who It's For

ModernDev is built for:

- Career changers who want to break into tech but don't know where to start
- Fresh IT/CS graduates who finished school but don't feel job-ready

## Business Model

ModernDev is a **subscription-based platform** with recurring monthly and annual plans. Learners pay for continued access to the full curriculum, AI interview simulator, and all future content updates.

Planned tiers:
- **Free** — limited access, sample lessons, and one roadmap preview
- **Pro** — full curriculum, all roadmaps, AI interview simulator, projects
- **Teams** *(future)* — for bootcamps and schools to use ModernDev with their students

## Philosophy

> *"The goal is not to produce code. The goal is to produce developers."*

ModernDev is built on the belief that the best way to learn is by doing — with structure, feedback, and just enough help to keep you moving without doing the thinking for you. AI is a tool, not a replacement for understanding. The developers who will thrive in the AI era are the ones who know what they're doing well enough to know when AI is wrong.
